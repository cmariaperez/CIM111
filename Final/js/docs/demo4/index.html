Why Black & White?

We live in a generation highly depenedant on color to enhance all forms of media and entertainment. From movies and videos, to advertisements and photography- color plays a big impact on graphics and visuals. But does this mean that a world without color diminishes the true form of art? In this blog, I hope to take you back to a time- as simpler time- a world of black and white.
